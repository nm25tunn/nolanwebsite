Emperor Palpatine

The Emperor is the supreme leader of the Galactic Empire. After manipulating the Republic Senate into giving him emergency powers he seized absolute power. He is also known as Darth Sidious the current reigning Dark Lord of the Sith and master to the menacing Darth Vader. Palpatine is known largely as one of the most powerful Sith and his ruthless style has helped elevate the Sith to total galactic control. He has a particular soft spot for large ball shaped superweapons.

Strengths

  • A master of deception, he was able to fool the Jedi Council and Galactic Senate into thinking he was just an old man.
  • Intelligence, he is known for setting up perfect plans that result in his adversaries destroying themselves.
  • Force Lightning, one of the most proficient users of the ability to ever live, it is said that those hit by his force are vaporized.

Weaknesses

  • His confidence, ultimately his undoing, the Sith Lord was undone by his inability to take the threat of the rebellion seriously.
  • The Dark Side, his abuse of the Dark Side of the force has tortured his body and leaves him dependent on it's constant use.
  • Darth Vader, The Chosen One of Jedi proficy Palpatine has kept Vader close to help protect himself from falling victim to fate.
