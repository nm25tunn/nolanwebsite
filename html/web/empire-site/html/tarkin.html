Grand Moff Tarkin

The governor of the outer rim, Tarkin is one of The Emperor's most trusted officials. Tarkin is a veteran of the clone wars, where he served as a commander within the Republic's military. He was the architect of the largest Imperial effort to destroy any hope of rebellion in the galaxy and was primarily responsible for Imperial efforts to crush the alliance in the earlier stage of the Galactic Civil War. He was commander of the first Death Star battlestation until his demise along with the battle station.

Strengths

  • Intelligence, his cunning and tactile approach to dealing with rebels allowed The Empire to suppress the galaxy for decades.
  • Ruthlessness, Tarkin's disregard for civillian casualties made him an effective force for projecting power.
  • Respected, his results have given him incredible pull in The Empire granting him resources and loyalty.

Weaknesses

  • His overconfidence, Tarkin's failure to realize threats resulted directly in his death at the battle of Yavin.
  • His diregard for civilians, while in short term situations his ruthlessness rewards him in the long run it has created countless foes for The Empire and has only emboldened the rebels.
  • The Force, Much like other Imperial officers he resfuses to acknowledge the power the force has and often discounts the threat it poses
