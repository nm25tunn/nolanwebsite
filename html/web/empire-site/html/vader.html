Darth Vader

The commander of the Imperial Army and imposing enforcer of The Emperor. Lord Vader is known for his harsh treatment of rebel operatives and his overall menace. The perpetrator of the destruction of the Jedi order he is known as one of the greatest Jedi killers to ever live. His proficiency in killing Jedi is often attributed to his training by the order as Anakin Skywalker in his youth. The sound of his mechanical breathing apparatus strikes fear in the hearts of dissenters the galaxy over.

Strengths

  • Physical strength, Anakin Skywalker was a strong young man and since his fall he has only become stronger with his new cybernetic limbs.
  • Ruthlessness, The Emperor's enforcer is known for his willingness to do anything regardless of cost to accomplish his mission.
  • The Force, The Chosen One was born with an unprecedented aptitude for force use and is considered one of the most powerful force users to ever live.

Weaknesses

  • His mechanical parts, from his limbs to his breather Lord Vader would die without his cybernetics which are vulnerable to failure.
  • His son, Vader has a particular soft spot for his family particularly his son who manages to shake his ironclad resolve.
  • Attrition, Vader's subordinates don't have a very long life expectancy highly capable officers have been killed by the Dark Lord for failing him.
